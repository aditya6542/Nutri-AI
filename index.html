About NutriAI

NutriAI is a deep-learning-inspired diet planning system implementing Neural Collaborative Filtering for personal food matching, LSTM-based meal timing logic, and Autoencoder-based taste profiling. Nutritional data references USDA FoodData Central and WHO Dietary Guidelines. Built as part of the B.Tech CSE Term Paper "AI Nutrition Recommendation Using Deep Learning" by Aditya Mehta, Amity University, Noida.

Version 2.0  ·  57 Features across 6 categories  ·  100% Free  ·  Data stored locally in browser  ·  No server, no tracking
